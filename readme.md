## D3 globe animation

Attempt to generate a world countries visualisation using D3

## Viewing ideas

Fly to a country. This is:

- the name of the country
- the flag
- the area
- the population
- neighbouring countries

## Training geography ideas

1. Fly to a country - "This is the name of the country you are observing"
2. Flag guess - "Which of these 4 flags belongs to the country"
3. Neighboring countries - "What are the neighboring counties"?
